Habitat and Natural Envoirnment

Bees are commonly inhabiting a variety of natural habitats, such as tropical areas, grasslands, woodlands, and deserts. They favor areas with a lot of flowering plants, like woodlands, gardens, orchards, and meadows. Bees require hollow trees or other cavities to build their nests.
